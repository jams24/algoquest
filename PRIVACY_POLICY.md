# Privacy Policy — AlgoQuest

**Last updated:** April 15, 2026

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for login)
- Username (displayed in app)
- Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)

### Usage Data
- Problem progress and quiz scores
- XP, level, and streak data
- Preferred programming language
- Daily goal preferences

### Third-Party Services
- **RevenueCat**: Processes in-app purchases. See [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).
- **Google Play**: Handles app distribution and payments.

## How We Use Your Data
- Track your progress across 150 NeetCode problems
- Maintain streaks and gamification features
- Process subscriptions and purchases
- Improve the app based on aggregate usage patterns

## Data Storage
- Account data stored on secure PostgreSQL servers
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt (industry standard)
- API communication encrypted via HTTPS

## Data Sharing
We do NOT sell your data. We share data only with:
- RevenueCat (subscription management)
- Google Play (app distribution)

## Your Rights
- Delete your account and all data by contacting us
- Export your progress data
- Opt out of non-essential data collection

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13.

## Contact
For privacy questions: privacy@algoquest.app

## Changes
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new date.
